    • To Do List

    The To-Do App is a simple task management tool designed to help users stay organized and productive. Built using React, this intuitive web application allows users to effortlessly create, manage, and track their tasks in real-time. I created this app in order to manage my own tasks and time while simultanously being an effective, yet common, method to showcase my skills and knowledge of the basics.

    The app allows you to easily add, edit, or remove tasks, and marking them as complete or incomplete is simple with just a click. The app automatically saves your changes, ensuring your progress is never lost for the next time you want to use it. The interface is designed to be intuitive, making navigation a breeze. Additionally, you have the flexibility to personalise your tasks with custom names. Whether you're tackling daily chores, managing work assignments, or planning long-term goals, the To-Do App offers a solution to help you stay on top of your tasks and achieve your objectives efficiently.
