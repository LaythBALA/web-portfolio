    • Pokédex

    Pokémon is a fundamental part of my childhood that has stuck with me till the present - I have always enjoyed and loved the concept, world-building, and of course, the Pokémon themselves. My passion and interest in Pokémon introduced me to several hobbies, including gaming and in turn programming.

    I decided to create a fun Pokédex web application that utilises the PokéAPI for data and displays it in an appealing UI design. The result? A captivating Pokédex web application that seamlessly integrates with the renowned PokéAPI to deliver a comprehensive exploration of the Pokémon world. This dynamic application offers users an interactive and visually appealing interface to delve into. Driven by a commitment to excellence and a deep-rooted love for Pokémon, I designed the app to delight users. With an intuitive user experience and striking visual elements, the Pokédex web application transforms data into a journey of nostalgia.
